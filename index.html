About
About Norchester University

Norchester University is committed to inclusive excellence in teaching and research. Our community blends tradition with innovation to prepare students for global impact. Founded in 2019, we serve 2,340 students across four schools with a focus on innovation, sustainability, and community impact.

🏛️

Four Schools

Arts & Sciences, Technology, Business, and Health Sciences.

🌐

Community Focus

Tight-knit campus with strong local partnerships and hands-on programs.

🏆

Growing Fast

One of the fastest-growing small universities in New England since 2019.
