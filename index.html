About
About Norchester University

Norchester University is committed to inclusive excellence in teaching and research. Our community blends tradition with innovation to prepare students for global impact. Founded in 1892, we serve over 18,000 students across six colleges and forty-two research centers worldwide.

🏛️

Six Colleges

Arts & Sciences, Engineering, Business, Law, Medicine, and Education.

🌐

Global Reach

Partner universities in 28 countries with semester exchange programs.

🏆

Rankings

Consistently ranked among the top 50 research universities in North America.
